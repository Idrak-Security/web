Cookie Policy of Idrak Security SRL

This policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to enhance your browsing experience.

Effective Date: June 6, 2025

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Idrak Security SRL uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website (www.idrak-security.com). By accessing or using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They store information about your browsing activity to enhance functionality, performance, and user experience.

Cookies can be first-party (set by our domain) or third-party (set by external services).

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary

Enable core site functionality

Performance / Analytics

Collect anonymous usage statistics

Functional

Remember user preferences

Marketing / Profiling

Track user behavior for targeted advertising

4. Managing and Disabling Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies via settings. You can remove or block cookies through your browser preferences. Be aware that blocking certain cookies may affect the website's functionality and user experience.

Browser Cookie Settings:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Apple Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Site permissions > Cookies and site data

5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, we display a cookie banner that explains our use of cookies and provides options for managing your preferences. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

You can modify your cookie preferences at any time through the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our website.

6. Cookie Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will post any updated version on our website with a new effective date.

8. Purpose of Data Collection

Cookies enhance your browsing experience by:

Authentication and Login Management

Keeping you logged in and maintaining your session

Analyzing usage patterns to improve performance

Marketing Cookies: Marketing cookies may be aggregated with broader datasets to deliver relevant ads; we do not sell your personal data.

9. Data Retention

Cookies remain on your device for varying durations, depending on their purpose:

Session Cookies Deleted when you close your browser
Persistent Cookies Remain until they expire or are manually deleted
Analytics Cookies Usually up to 24 months
Functional Cookies Up to 12 months
Marketing Cookies Up to 24 months
